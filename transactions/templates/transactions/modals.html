Overview

The Finances application is a complete individual or household bookkeeping sytem, including:

1) Income Statement,
2) Balance Sheet,
3) Cash Flow Statement,
4) Expense Analsys, and
5) Budgeting.

The application features antomated transaction capture & upload from your bank statement. The set of books is seeded with over 130 accounts, with predefined financial statemens, sections and lines. The "Setup Books" tab allows you to add, delete or update accounts or financial statements that come pre-seeded with TRAXX. The "Bookkeeping" tab allows you to upload transactions from your bank financial statements, enter manual transactions, or Post current net income to retained earnings. Moving Net earnings from the income statement to the balance sheet. The "Reporting" tab allows you to run any of your financial statements and optionally download the report.
