Overview
The Lists & Planning application serves multiple purposes. Like all TRAXX applications Lists & Planning may be easily run on a hand-held device such as a mobile phone. It is a trivial matter to create and maintain simple lists such as a Grocery List, Chores, To-Do's, book lists, journaling and random notes. Lists may be shared with family members and a single list, may have multiple authors, or contributors.

Lists & Planning also supports simple project planning, with defined predecessor/successor relationships and is perfect for planning vacations, business planning, and personal development plans. Planning fully supports the SMART goal method, where you apply specific, measurable, achievable, relevant and time-bound objectives to help you reach your goals and track progress.
