A Story of Success
The TRAXX Resource & Communication Management System was created by Kirk Shelton the founder and Managing Principal of "Cliffs Software Solutions" (CSS). Kirk has extensive experience building and managing startup technology concerns and the innovative use of technology to develop and deliver exceptional value to clients. His experience includes business development, successfully delivering large, complex, multi-disciplined projects, developing and delivering custom software solutions and integrations, and building feature rich extensions to enhance existing platform software solutions.
M.B.A. Economics and Finance, Honors, Wayne State University, Detroit, MI
B.S. Management Information Systems, Honors, Oakland University, Rochester Hills, MI

                                          The CSS Board of Advisors Includes: Edward J. Sanz, Michael Onak, Rui Sousa, and Lisa Shelton.

Edward J. Sanz, CFA, CTP
Managing Partner, ABTV
Mr. Sanz is a senior finance and restructuring professional with over thirty years of experience as a CFO, interim manager, and financial consultant to business owners, boards of directors and senior management in the U.S. and abroad. He manages a consulting firm that advises companies requiring transformation or solving complex financial and operational problems.
He began his career in commercial lending and public accounting and has since been in senior financial management roles and an advisor to business owners, company management and boards of directors. Mr. Sanz has authored articles for industry journals and chapters of the Turnaround Management Association's Body of Knowledge and has spoken on a number of related topics. He currently serves on the Board of the Carolinas Chapter of the Turnaround Management Association (TMA) where he was also a past President. He also served on the Board of Trustees of TMA Global, where he chaired the Certification Oversight Committee which oversees the administration of the Certified Turnaround Professional (CTP) designation. Mr. Sanz graduated from the University of Virginia with a Bachelor of Science in Commerce and received his Master's in Business Administration with honors from Emory University. He holds Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) and Certified Turnaround Professional (CTP) designations.

Michael Onak
Senior Partner (retired)
Ernst & Young LLP Michael Onak ran the Banking Consulting practice in the Americas. He has been in professional services for over 30 years focused primarily on the Financial Services Industry. Michael has served E&Y's largest clients and built and grown large consulting practices. He has held many Americas and global leadership positions at E&Y.
Mr. Onak Graduated from Northern Illinois University with a Bachelor of Science Degree

Rui Sousa
Lead Engineer & Software Development Manager, Cliffs Software Solutions, LLC In addition to being an international advisor to Cliffs Software Solutions, Mr. Sousa is a talented, experienced software developer with extensive Python, Django, and Java Script experience. He has been integral to the early development of TRAXX with a strong emphasis on best practices, object orientation and reusability.
B.A. Computer Science, University of Cabo Verde, Praia Cabo Verde

Lisa Shelton
Lisa is an experienced IT and Accounting professional having run multiple IT and Accounting departments. She has been successful in multiple enterprise package selections and implementations. Lisa brings the "Practical Magic" to TRAXX with a heavy emphasis on the user interface, simplicity and useability.

Valuing growth means that we have the drive to continuously improve both ourselves and our company. Growth is based on mutual success. CSS is a company that develops its staff and provides an environment that fosters personal and professional development.

ACCOUNTABILITY: Is a willingness to accept and embrace responsibility for one's actions and the impact on the enterprise.

INNOVATION: Challenging ourselves to see what is possible to better meet the needs of fellow associates, clients and company. CSS will always be a work in progress, striving to improve and do better, thinking of creative ways to solve tough problems and take calculated risks. Finding new ways to solve a problem can help the company move forward.
