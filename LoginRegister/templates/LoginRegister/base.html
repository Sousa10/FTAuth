Overview
TRAXX by Cliffs Software Solutions LLC is an Individual Resource & Communication Management system. The system consists of six applications:

                                                                                                                Finances
                                                                                                                Calendar
                                                                                                                Lists & Planning
                                                                                                                Communication
                                                                                                                Fitness & Sports, and
                                                                                                                Administration.

The applications are orchestrated to work together, with a similar look, feel and navigation model. Each application includes a set of self explanatory tutorials which describe how to use and get the most out of each appplication. The applications are designed to be "ready for use" without extensive configuration. In the exceptional case where setup or configuration is required, such as Finances, a pre-developed model template is provided which may easily be tailored to meet the needs of the individual user.
The Finances Application
The FINANCES application is a complete individual or household bookkeeping system.

First one sets up the books. This includes defining the reports, including the layouts and rollup lines which will appear on each report. Then defining the accounts which are used to capture transactions and are then summarized in the rollups for each financial report. The system comes pre-seeded with a complete set of reports, rollups and accounts which may simply be tweaked a bit for individual use if so desired.

Once the Set of Books is prepared and tailored for individual use, you are ready to begin bookkeeping. Bank Credit Card Statements can easily be downloaded from the Bank and quickly uploaded into Finances. Manual enteries may also be entered as needed such as when buying a major asset such as a car or new home, to capture those those transactions in FINANCES as well. Most commonly however, you will simply be uploading monthly expenses and income. Periodically, one may post periodic Net Income/Loss to retained earnings. This will clear current revenue and expenses and move the summary value to the balance sheet. Unlike standard Accounting systems, this may be done on one's own schedule, monthly, quarterly, yearly or any period of time as desired.

Reporting may be run at any time. Available Financial Reports include:

Income Statement
Balance Sheet
Cash Flow Statement
Expense Analysis
Budget Performance

One may easily Drill-Down to what accounts make up a statement line. And, for each account, what transactions constitute the account balance.
The Calendar Application
The Calendar application is integrated with Microsoft Outlook, Google Calendar, and Apple Calendar. Integration can be configured to be bi-directional, or inbound only. In this manner, the TRAXX Calendar may be one's central calendaring system with both personal and professional events all in one place without the need to publish personal events, meetings, and plans.

TRAXX Calendar includes all standard calendar functionality and allows one to schedule events and meetings, define venues, and identify event participants.
The Lists & Planning Application
The Lists & Planning application serves multiple purposes. Like all TRAXX applications Lists & Planning may be easily run on a hand-held device such as a mobile phone. It is a trivial matter to create and maintain simple lists such as a Grocery List, Chores, To-Do's, book lists, journaling and random notes. Lists may be shared with family members and a single list, may have multiple authors, or contributors.

Lists & Planning also supports simple project planning, with defined predecessor/successor relationships and is perfect for planning vacations, business planning, and personal development plans. Planning fully supports the SMART goal method, where you apply specific, measurable, achievable, relevant and time-bound objectives to help you reach your goals and track progress.
The Communication Application
The Communication Application is highly secure and controlled social media application perfect for keeping in touch with Friends and family. One defines who they want to follow and stay in touch with. One may then broadcast text "Meeps" and pictures for followers to consume.

Hashtags are also supported. Hashtags are words or phrases without spaces that begin with the hashtag symbol (#) and help users find content related to a given topic. They can be used to group and categorize tweets related to a specific topic, event, place, theme, thing, verb, or industry term. One may create their own unique hashtags for specific campaigns or long-term branding, and make sure they are snappy, memorable, and not already used or negative.

Of course, the Communication Application also supports re-meeping which is to re-publish the meeps of others.
The Fitness & Sports Application
The Fitness & Sports Application allows one to define excercises, define workouts, capture workout performances, and review performances over time to measure progress and improvement.

Workouts may be organized by various attributes such as Upper Body, Lower Body, Core, Home Workout and Gym workout.

One may also use the Fitness & Sports Application to capture performances in various sports such as Golf, Tennis, Pickleball, Mountain BIking, Swimming, Pilates, Yoga, and running.

For each sport, one may define the venue, such as the golf course, capture performances, and view performances and progress over time.
The Administration Application
The Administration Application allows one to define integrations, share Application access with other family members and establish desired security and privacy controls.
